- aspect-ratio corresponds to viewport aspect ratio (window.innerWidth/window.innerHeight)
- resolution in dpi tends to be similar to DPR (but slightly smaller) multiplied by x100
- resolution in dppx is the replacement to dpi and corresponds to DPR
